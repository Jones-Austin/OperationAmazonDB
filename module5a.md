## Module 5A
### Business Analytics Problem:
The goal is to identify which products are most popular and generate the highest revenue in the system.
### Available Data:
The system collects data from users, products, orders, and order_items tables. This includes product names, prices, and quantities purchased.
### Data Analysis Approach:
We analyze total sales and revenue per product using SQL queries. The system calculates how many times each product was purchased and the total revenue generated.
### Visualization:
The data can be visualized using bar charts to display product popularity and revenue trends.
### Solution Design:
The system provides insights into customer purchasing behavior by identifying top-selling products. This helps improve inventory management, marketing strategies, and pricing decisions.
